Trading Execution Mechanics

1. Market Scanning

Bot scans 1,000 Polymarket markets every 6 hours using Polygon RPC nodes.

  • Filters for markets with ≥93% probability
  • Maximum 60 days to resolution
  • Minimum $500 trading volume
  • Identifies bond-like opportunities

2. Opportunity Analysis

Each opportunity undergoes rigorous risk assessment.

  • Quarter-Kelly position sizing
  • $200 maximum per position
  • 10 maximum concurrent positions
  • 5% daily loss limit

3. Trade Execution

Automated execution via Polymarket CLOB using smart contracts.

  • Direct contract interactions on Polygon
  • Gas optimization for cost efficiency
  • Real-time price verification
  • Slippage protection (max 2%)

4. Risk Management

Continuous monitoring and automated risk controls.

  • ATR-based trailing stops
  • Portfolio concentration limits
  • Daily P&L tracking
  • Emergency stop triggers
